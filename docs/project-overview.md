Project Specification

1. Introduction

This document presents the business and technical requirement for the design and implementation of the enterprise network for StoneCraft Ltd., a tile manufacturing subsidiary. The projects focus is to deliver a scalable, resilient and secure campus network that supports the company's day-to-day operations. The network is desidned to provide room for future growth and integration with the parent company's headquaters.

The projects objectives, scope, and requirement are established in this document. It serves as the primary reference for the network design, implementation, testing and future enhancements documented all through this repository.

2. Company Background

Stonecraft Ltd. is a tile manufacturing company in Nigeria with approximately 250 staffs accross various departments. The company operates a manufacturing plant alongside several adminstrative offices within a single campus environment.

Keeping in mind future growth in production and business operations, the company requires a modern enterprise network that is capable of supporting office users, machines, IP cameras, shared printers and local servers. The network must provide reliable Internet connectivity, high availability, and a scalable architecture.

This project focuses exclusively on the design and implementation of the subsidiary's campus network. Integration with the parent company's headquarter's will be considered in future project phases.
